How it Works

  • Grow your own virtual plants!
  • Contribute to the water conservation efforts in the city by completing quests.
  • Once you complete quests, you get a certain amount of xp
  • Once you get enough xp, you will level up, and get a shiny new plant graphic!
  • Remember, it's all about conserving water, so it doesn't work out unless you actually complete the quests!
  • Stay tuned for more cool features!
